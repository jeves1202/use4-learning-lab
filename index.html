USE4 Learning Lab

A from-scratch, USE4-style US equity factor risk model — the assembly line from raw vendor CSVs to a portfolio risk forecast

σp2 = w⊤XFX⊤w + w⊤Δw

the governing identity — factor risk plus specific risk; everything below serves it

An educational reconstruction of the Barra USE4 US equity risk model, built from Sharadar and Fama–French data and the published methodology notes. Each numbered step ships a spec and withholds the build — writing the build notebook is the point. The textbook is the answer key.

GitHub repo·README·Usage guide

How this works. Work the steps in order. For each one, read the spec (under Labs), write the build notebook yourself, and check your numbers against the matching textbook chapter (under Textbook) — its Measured boxes come from a real build run and are targets to reproduce in shape, not vintage. Every step ends with an explicit PASS/FAIL validation battery.
