\section{Purpose and Scope}

Audits the Growth factor. USE4 weights (Appendix~A, p.~52):
$0.70\,\mathrm{EGRLF} + 0.20\,\mathrm{EGRO} + 0.10\,\mathrm{SGRO}$; with no
analyst-forecast data in Sharadar, EGRLF is proxied by EGRO, giving effective
$\mathrm{GRO} = 0.90\,\mathrm{EGRO} + 0.10\,\mathrm{SGRO}$ --- 5-year normalised
OLS slopes of EPS and SPS.

\smallskip
\textbf{Scope.} This audit covers every pipeline stage of
\texttt{gro\_build.ipynb} from the lab's reference run of 2026-06-11: all printed diagnostics, the
validation battery, and the output parquet schema. All numerical values are
verbatim from the executed notebook. The notebook generates no figures.

\NOTE{Reference numbers: every value in this document comes verbatim from the lab's reference run of 2026-06-11 (Sharadar data through 2026-06-10). Your rebuild will differ in row counts and tail statistics with data vintage; distribution shapes, stability coefficients, and check outcomes should match. Treat persistent sign or order-of-magnitude differences as bugs in your build until proven otherwise.}

%──────────────────────────────────
\subsection{Stage 4 --- GRO estimator}

\begin{description}[style=nextline]
  \item[PDF SPEC] Normalised 5-year OLS slope of EPS (and SPS).
  \item[Implementation] PIT cross-join of annual filings to signal dates; per-(stock, date) OLS slope / mean; EGRO/SGRO pre-clipped to $\pm5$; composite $0.90/0.10$.
  \item[Observed]\phantom{x}
    \begin{itemize}[nosep, topsep=-\parskip]
      \item PIT cross-join: \textbf{27{,}039{,}371} rows; aggregated ($n\geq3$): \textbf{2{,}898{,}336}
      \item Pre-clipped: EGRO \textbf{41{,}815} rows, SGRO \textbf{1{,}673} rows
      \item Panel: \textbf{2{,}898{,}336} rows, \textbf{315} dates, \textbf{13{,}452} tickers; duplicates \textbf{0}; GRO null rate 1.8\%
      \item Spot 2026-05-29: \textbf{13{,}445} tickers, median gro \textbf{0.0096}
    \end{itemize}
  \item[Status] \PASS{MATCHES SPEC}
\end{description}

%──────────────────────────────────
\subsection{Stage 5 --- Outlier trim (3$\sigma$)}

\begin{description}[style=nextline]
  \item[PDF SPEC] Methodology \S2.2 trim.
  \item[Implementation] CW mean $\pm\,3\cdot$EW std (ESTU bounds, applied to all).
  \item[Observed]\phantom{x}
    \begin{itemize}[nosep, topsep=-\parskip]
      \item Clipped (ESTU): \textbf{18{,}507} / 674{,}821 = \textbf{2.74\%} (target 0.5--2\%)
      \item Bounds: lo mean $-2.377$; hi mean $2.128$
      \item Post-trim at 2026-05-29 (ESTU, $n=2{,}551$): mean $-0.0394$, std 0.5622, skew $-0.242$, kurt 7.780
    \end{itemize}
  \item[Deviation] \WARN{ESTU clip rate 2.74\% sits 0.74pp above the 0.5--2\% band --- growth slopes are fat-tailed even after the $\pm5$ pre-clip. Accepted: tightening the pre-clip would distort genuine high-growth names.}
  \item[Status] \WARN{PARTIAL MATCH} (clip rate above band, documented)
\end{description}
